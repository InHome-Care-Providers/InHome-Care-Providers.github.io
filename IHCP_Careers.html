Director of Patient Services
Greater Atlanta Area Full-Time Leadership

Senior leadership role overseeing operational and clinical coordination of home health services. You'll provide strategic direction, staff leadership, and operational oversight to the Care Coordination team.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead, supervise, and mentor the Care Coordination team including intake staff and scheduling personnel
  • Oversee patient intake, referral management, scheduling, authorization, and care coordination
  • Develop and evaluate departmental workflows, policies, and procedures
  • Monitor KPIs including referral conversion rates, start-of-care timeliness, and patient satisfaction
  • Champion compassionate, patient-centered service delivery across the department

Qualifications

  • Senior-level experience in home health operations or patient services leadership
  • Strong knowledge of Medicare/Medicaid home health regulations
  • Proven ability to lead teams and drive operational performance

Greater Atlanta Area Full-Time Patient Services

Central point of contact for patients, families, referral sources, and clinical staff. You'll ensure home healthcare services are initiated, scheduled, and delivered seamlessly from referral through completion of care.

  • Manage visit schedules for nurses, therapists, and home health aides

Qualifications

Physical Therapist (PT)
Greater Atlanta Area Full-Time Therapy

Evaluate patients in their home environment, design individualized treatment plans, and deliver evidence-based physical therapy interventions that restore function, reduce pain, and improve mobility.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform comprehensive PT evaluations assessing strength, ROM, balance, gait, and functional mobility
  • Develop goal-oriented plans of care with patients, families, and the interdisciplinary team
  • Provide skilled services including therapeutic exercise, neuromuscular re-education, balance/gait training, and manual therapy
  • Complete OASIS assessments as required by Medicare home health guidelines
  • Design and instruct patients in safe home exercise programs

Qualifications

  • Current Georgia PT license (or eligible)
  • Home health experience preferred; new graduates welcome
  • OASIS proficiency preferred

Personal Care Assistant (PCA)
Greater Atlanta Area Full-Time / Part-Time Home Care

Provide non-medical personal care, companionship, and assistance with activities of daily living in clients' homes. Ideal for individuals passionate about serving others and supporting client independence and dignity.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist clients with bathing, grooming, oral care, dressing, and personal hygiene
  • Support safe mobility, transfers, and ambulation using proper body mechanics
  • Help with meal preparation and adherence to dietary guidelines
  • Provide medication reminders (PCAs do not administer medications)
  • Perform light housekeeping and provide meaningful companionship

Qualifications

  • Compassionate, dependable, and committed to client dignity
  • CNA certification or prior caregiving experience preferred
  • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
